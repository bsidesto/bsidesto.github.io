Intercast Staffing will be running a Resume Village at BSides Toronto on October 6th, 2019!

About this Event

Our wonderful sponsor Intercast Staffing will be available on Sunday October 6th hepling to build solid resumes, setting up mock interviews, and providing general guidance for finding jobs in the Information Security industry. Whether you are just starting out, or you are a veteran looking for a change, bring a resume and try things out.

If you would like to try a mock interview, book some time with Intercast Staffing at their table in the morning, and the interviews will be conducted in the afternoon, after DarkSides.

You can find current job listings here. If you have any general job related questions, you can email them at info@intercaststaffing.com.
