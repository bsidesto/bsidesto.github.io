About

The BSides are coming back to Toronto for another year!

On November 7th join us for a day of security talks, conversations, and other hijinks. we will be holding a day of single track security talks.

While maintaining a low key environment, we will bring you high quality talks in a laid back atmosphere.

Follow us on twitter for the announcement when we open registration.
