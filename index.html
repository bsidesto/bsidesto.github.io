ABOUT


The BSides are coming to Toronto!

On October 5th join us for a day of security talks, conversations, pool, and other hijinks. We will be occupying the Monarch Tavern for a day of single track security talks. Our CFP is open until Sept 15th and we encourage you to participate

While maintaining a low key environment, we will bring you high quality talks in a laid back atmosphere. A chill-out area with a live stream of the talks will also be available for those who want to socialize during the day.

Night time activities are still being arranged. More info to come!

Please see the CFP page if you're interested in submitting a talk.

Our registration is open now.



CFP


On Oct 5th, 2013 the BSides are coming to Toronto! We’re accepting talks of 25 and 55 minutes long on a number of topics. We will also have room for lighting talks, so if you have something that isn’t right for a full-length talk submit it anyway.

The conference is one track and will take place over one day, followed by a party in the evening. We’re looking for security talks that are cool, weird, new, or just random and interesting. We’re open to talks on any security related topic, but preference will be given to original technical talks from the Toronto security community.

Talks will be reviewed and accepted by September 15th.

Topics that we really dig:

  • New ways to hack things
  • Strange hardware and software that you have broken
  • New approaches to defense
  • New tricks in web application hacking
  • Hardware hacking
  • Anything else you think is cool

What to send us:

  • Title of the talk
  • Name / handle / twitter / website
  • Length
  • Abstract
  • Why it’s interesting
  • Have you presented it before?
  • And if you already have them, slides and / or technical papers

Please send your submission to cfp@bsidesto.ca
