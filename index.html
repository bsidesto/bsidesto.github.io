About

The BSides are coming back to Toronto for another year!

On November 7th join us for a day of security talks, conversations, and other hijinks. we will be holding a day of single track security talks. Our CFP is open and we encourage you to participate.

While maintaining a low key environment, we will bring you high quality talks in a laid back atmosphere.

Our registration will open June 15th, follow us on twitter for the announcement.
